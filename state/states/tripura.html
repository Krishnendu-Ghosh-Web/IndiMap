Tripura

Tripura, located in northeastern India, is known for its rich cultural heritage and picturesque landscapes.
The state is renowned for its ancient temples, such as the Tripura Sundari Temple, and its vibrant festivals, including Durga Puja and Kharchi.
Tripura's natural beauty is highlighted by its lush green hills, serene lakes, and the majestic Neermahal Palace, which is situated in the middle of a lake.
The state also boasts a diverse range of flora and fauna in its wildlife sanctuaries and national parks.
