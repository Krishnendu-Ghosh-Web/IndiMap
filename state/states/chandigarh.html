Chandigarh

Chandigarh, the capital of both Punjab and Haryana, is a city renowned for its modern architecture and urban planning.
Designed by the architect Le Corbusier, the city features a unique grid layout and numerous green spaces.
Notable landmarks include the Capitol Complex, the Rock Garden, and Sukhna Lake.
Chandigarh's blend of modernity and nature offers a distinctive urban experience, with well-planned sectors and vibrant cultural life.
Its status as a union territory allows it to serve as a key administrative and cultural hub in northern India.
