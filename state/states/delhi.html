Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history and modern flair.
The city is renowned for its historic monuments, bustling markets, and diverse cuisine.
Famous landmarks include the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar,
while areas like Connaught Place and Chandni Chowk offer a blend of colonial and traditional experiences.
Delhi's role as a political, cultural, and economic hub makes it a dynamic and historic destination.
